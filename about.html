Our Story

Starting from my back garden washing my own dog I knew I wanted to extend that same care I had for my own dog towards other people's dogs as well. That is when I decided that I would take it seriously full time and begin to show others that their dog is my own when it is in my care.


After saving up for 3 years from grooming many dogs in my own back garden to other people's houses I then made enough to purchase my own place and fully commit to the vision I had in mind when I first set out on this journey.


Now Dog Groomers has grown as a hub for pets to refresh themselves with the care that they deserve and need inside of a fully dedicated and modern dog grooming facility.
